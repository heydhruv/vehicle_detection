About Us

Our project aims to develop an intelligent system for detecting and counting vehicles in parking areas The system uses advanced computer vision techniques to analyze the video footage captured by the cameras placed at strategic locations in parking areas. The goal of the system is to provide accurate and real-time information about the availability of parking spaces.The vehicle detection and counting system is designed to work in all weather conditions and can handle high traffic volumes.The system uses a combination of machine learning algorithms and image processing techniques to identify and track vehicles.The system is capable of detecting and counting various types of vehicles, including cars, trucks, buses, and motorcycles.
